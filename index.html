How UniDesk Works

UniDesk is a comprehensive platform designed for university students to organize, share, and study their academic materials efficiently. Our AI-powered system helps you maximize your learning potential.

📁

Upload & Organize

Students can easily upload PDFs, notes, PPTs, and other study materials for their semester subjects. Materials are organized by branches and semesters for easy access.

🤖

AI-Powered Assistant

Our intelligent AI assistant provides instant summaries of your uploaded materials, generates practice questions for exams, and offers personalized study recommendations.

🏛️

University Structure

Content is systematically organized by university branches and semesters, making it easy to find relevant materials for your specific course and academic level.

📝

Exam Preparation

Generate custom quizzes, get important topic summaries, and receive AI-generated questions based on your uploaded study materials to ace your exams.

🔍

Smart Search

Quickly find specific topics, concepts, or information across all your uploaded materials using our intelligent search functionality.

📊

Progress Tracking

Monitor your study progress, track completed topics, and get insights into your learning patterns to optimize your study schedule.
